To best understand Floe, it's helpful to know exactly what we mean when we mention specific terms:

- **[Package](../installation/install-packages#what-is-a-package)** — the portable distribution container used to download and install Floe content (`.floe-pkg`, `.floe-pkg-enc`, or `.zip`). Packages may contain sample libraries, presets, or both.
- **[Sample Library](../reference/sample-libraries)** — a collection of mapped, playable sounds along with images, tags, and other metadata. Sample libraries provide the instruments and impulse responses that Floe loads.
- **[Instrument](../reference/instruments)** — a playable sound source loaded into one of Floe's 3 layers, such as a sampled piano or an ambient drone (provided by a sample library).
- **[Impulse Response](../usage/effects#convolution-reverb)** — a sample that determines the character of Floe's Convolution Reverb effect (provided by a sample library).
- **[Preset](../usage/presets)** — a production-ready snapshot of all of Floe's parameters and options, along with a name, description, and tags.
