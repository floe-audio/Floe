We've just released [Taiko Drums](/packages/taiko-drums), a free multisampled Japanese taiko percussion library for Floe. This is our port of [S. Christian Collins' excellent SCC Taiko Drums](https://www.schristiancollins.com/percussion).

<!-- truncate -->

The library includes 15 different drums in a single instrument, each with multiple velocity layers and round-robin sampling. **Taiko Drums 1 and 2** (C2-C#2 and D2-D#2) are the highlights - they use the most samples and deliver the most natural, authentic taiko tone.

Each drum is mapped to a pair of adjacent keys (white key + black key), making it easy to play realistic drum rolls by trilling between the two notes.

Taiko Drums is completely free with attribution required. Download from the [Taiko Drums package page](/packages/taiko-drums).
