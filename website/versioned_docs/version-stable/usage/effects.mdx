## Notable effects
### Convolution Reverb
Floe's convolution reverb effect has numerous impulse responses (IRs) that explore both realism and sound-design. The IRs are selected using one of Floe's powerful _[browsers](../key-concepts/browsers)_, where IRs can be sourced from installed sample libraries that choose to provide them.

### Delay and reverb
Floe's reverb and delay are very high-quality, originating from Vital's pro-standard effects rack for excellent space design.
