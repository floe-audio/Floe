# Glossary

> {/*

To best understand Floe, it's helpful to know exactly what we mean when we mention specific terms:

-   **[Package](/docs/beta/installation/install-packages#what-is-a-package)** — the portable distribution container used to download and install Floe content (`.floe-pkg`, `.floe-pkg-enc`, or `.zip`). Packages may contain sample libraries, presets, or both.
-   **[Sample Library](/docs/beta/reference/sample-libraries)** — a collection of mapped, playable sounds along with images, tags, and other metadata. Sample libraries provide the _instruments_ and _impulse responses_ that Floe loads.
-   **[Instrument](/docs/beta/reference/instruments)** — a playable sound source loaded into one of Floe's 3 layers, such as a sampled piano or an ambient drone (provided by a sample library).
-   **[Impulse Response](/docs/beta/usage/effects#convolution-reverb)** — a sample that determines the character of Floe's Convolution Reverb effect (provided by a sample library).
-   **[Preset](/docs/beta/usage/presets)** — a production-ready snapshot of all of Floe's parameters and options, along with a name, description, and tags.
